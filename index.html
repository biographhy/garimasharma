About

Garima as

Garima Sharma Timilsina(गरिमा शर्मा तिमिल्सिना) is a Nepali actress, influencer & model who primarily appears in Nepali Music Videos. She has featured in over 80 videos. She is known for acting/performance in most of the popular Nepali Music Videos Naboli Naboli, Din Gin Gin, Jeewan Fulne Mero Lahara, Mahashush and popular Nepali TV show Sakkigoni.


The contribution and popularity gained by her are one of the remarkable aspects. This is because to have time for performance in several music videos at the nominal age of life and short period of time which is one of the notable and revolutionary parts of her life. She has shared the screen on music videos with the different hit celebrities of Nepal. In fact, music video is another way that makes to become more recognisable among the public. Although her three prevoius videos were not much recognised among mass audience, Bhur Bhur Putali with Puspa Khadka played by her that stretches her popularity not over the TikTok World but also in the whole Nepali music industry.


She has played a numerious music videos with different co-actors like Rajesh Hamal, Dilip Rayamajhi, Paul Shah, Akash Shrestha and many more. She is one of the youngest ladies or actresses from Nepal with such decent craze and fan following.


Garima is one of the biggest fans of Pradeep Khadka. With a great interest in watching Nepali movies, listening songs, her aim also moves towards the movies industries. It means she had set a goal to be an actress and share the screen with her favorite and dream heroes.



Early life

Sharma was born born on 31 October 2001 (१५ कार्तिक , २०५८) in Beni, Myagdi, Nepal. Her parents are Gobinda Timilsina and Goma Sharma professionally both teachers. She has one sibling, a younger brother Gaurav Timilsina. Her family encourage, motivate and always stand together as backbone of her career in every situation as she is the only one in glamour field from her entire family branch and background. She gives all the credit to family for her success & greatful for huge sacrifice they have done for her. The family shiftefd to Bakulahar, Tandi, Chitwan for better education quality, opportunities and facilities. From her childhood, she seems very cute, simple, and ordinary girl. In terms of the Education part, her academic history was excellent since chidhood as she was passionate & hard-working girl. Her previous life was totally focused on education and study. She used to attend the better ranking on the school examinations.


Garima's mind is equally tender and clean, knowing how to treat everyone well. Growing up in a Nepali social culture from an early age, she has a keen interest in human service too. Later she completed her 10th standard (SEE) in 2017 from Skyrider Higher Secondary English Boarding School, Tandi securing A+(3.75 GPA). She also participated in beauty pageant compitition, Miss SLC Nepal 2017. Although she wasn’t able to grab the title, she achieved award of Miss Personality. She studied Science Stream in 10+2 from Skyrider College, Tandi with satisfying result securing A+ with 3.46 GPA. She's currently studying BBS in Madan Bhandari Memorial College, Anamnagar, Kathmandu. She also has keen interest in Law sector and its enforcement.



Garima Sharma As A Multi-Talent Girl

She ha no limit of her talent besides music videos. Her hobbies are travelling, singing and making new friends. Likewise, she is good at public speaking. From childhood, she used to talk in different debates and public speaking activities. She also worked few years as radio journalist in Radio Arpan operating a news program named 'Bollywood Crack'. She used to act in plays, write scripts, dance/sing and participate in oratory as well as win awards & prizes while still in school.

Career
Garima has used TikTok since 2018. From that time she used to play on a video over the music. She debuted her music video career with Kulendra Bishowkarma's Nepali Lok Dohori song Pirati ko Yeuta Git on Baisakh 07, 2076. Now she becomes one of the leading and rising artists of Nepal with enough fan following. She furnished her acting skills and later she started getting offered to music videos. She was able to do a lot of projects in a short period of time due to her hard work, talent and good fortune. Recently, she has involved in a huge project, the first ever video to shot in Khaptad National Park. There's not a single second, audience are disappointed with her performance except some criticism & minor controversies. She has given her best. She moved to Kathmandu to begin modeling & acting professionally. Recently she has ben featured in Nari & Navanari Magazine.
