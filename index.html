About

Garima as

Garima Sharma Timilsina (गरिमा शर्मा तिमिल्सिना) is a Nepali actress, social media influencer & model who primarily appears in Nepali Movie, numerous muisc videos & modeling sector. She has featured in over 120 videos upto date. She is known for acting/performance in most of the popular Nepali Videos Naboli Naboli, Din Gin Gin, Euta Sapana Chha and popular Nepali TV shows Bhadragol & Sakkigoni.


The contribution and popularity gained by her are one of the remarkable aspects. This is because to have time for performance in several music videos at the nominal age of life and short period of time which is one of the notable and revolutionary parts of her life. She has shared screen with the different hit celebrities of Nepal. In fact, music video is another way that makes to become more perceptible among the public. Although her three previous videos were not much noticed among mass audience, Bhur Bhur Putali with Puspa Khadka stretches her popularity not only over TikTok World but also in the whole Nepali music industry.


She has collaborated in numerous projects with different well-recognised actors like Rajesh Hamal, Dilip Rayamajhi, Akash Shrestha, Paul Shah and many more. She is one of the youngest ladies or actresses from Nepal with such decent craze and fan following.


With a great interest in watching Nepali movies, listening songs, her aim was towards the movies industries. It means she had set a goal to be an actress and share the screen with her favorite and dream heroes in movies. After a lot of hard word & devotion, she finally got offer to act in London To Paris movie. Her favourite hero since childhood as an audience is Pradeep Khadka.



Early life

Sharma was born on 31 October 2001 (१५ कार्तिक , २०५८) in Beni, Myagdi, Nepal. Her parents are Gobinda Timilsina and Goma Sharma professionally both teachers. She has one sibling, a younger brother Gaurav Timilsina. Her family encourage, motivate and always stand together as backbone of her career in every circumstances as she is the only one in glamour field from her entire family branch and background. She gives all the credit to family for gaining success & greatful for huge sacrifice they have done in order to brighten their daughter's future. Although their ancestral home is in Ramja, Parbat, the family shiftefd to Bakulahar, Tandi, Chitwan for better education quality, opportunities and facilities. From her childhood, she seems very cute, simple and ordinary girl. In terms of the Education part, her academic history was excellent since her childhood as she was passionate & hard-working girl. Her previous life was totally focused on education and studies. She used to attend the better ranking on the school examinations too.


Garima's mind is equally tender and clean, knowing how to treat everyone well. Growing up in a Nepali social culture from an early age, she has a keen interest in human service too. Later she completed her 10th standard (SEE) in 2017 from Skyrider Higher Secondary English Boarding School, Tandi securing A+(3.75 GPA). She also participated in beauty pageant compitition, Miss SLC Nepal 2017. Although she wasn’t able to grab the title, she achieved award of Miss Personality. She studied Science Stream in 10+2 from Skyrider College, Tandi with satisfying result securing A with 3.46 GPA. She's currently pursuing BBS in Madan Bhandari Memorial College, Anamnagar, Kathmandu. She also has keen interest in Law sector and its enforcement.



Garima Sharma As A Multi-Talent Girl

She has no limit of her talent besides music videos. Her hobbies are travelling, eating, singing, reading motivational books, vlogging & increasing network with wise-minded people around her. Likewise, she is good at public speaking. From childhood, she used to talk in different debates and programs. She also worked few years as radio journalist in Radio Arpan operating entertainment news program named 'Bollywood Crack'. She used to act in plays, write scripts, dance, sing, participate in oratory as well as win awards & prizes while still in school.

Career
Garima has used TikTok since 2018. From that time she used to play on a video over the music. She debuted her video career with Kulendra Bishowkarma's Nepali Lok Dohori song 'Pirati ko Yeuta Git' on Baisakh 07, 2076. Her debut movie in career is "London To Paris" which was shot in different places of England like London, Tower Bridge, Trafalgar Square, Wembley, Greenwood, Heathrow & polular places around which is releasing soon within this year's end. Now she becomes one of the leading and rising artists of Nepal with enough fan following. She furnished her act, dance and later she started getting offered to music videos. 5 ft. 6" tall Garima was able to present her skills in a short period of time due to hard work, strong determination, internal talent and good fortune. There's not a single second, audience are disappointed with her performance except some criticism & minor controversies, which are common in this sector. She has given her best. She shifted Kathmandu to begin her modeling & acting professionally. Recently she has been featured in Nari & Navanari Magazine too. Recently, the auspicious moment of the movie London to Paris happened on July 8, 2022. The filming was started in Greenwood, London and wrapped up in around one month. Directed by Ghanashyam Lamichhane, the film stars Samragyee RL Shah, Garima Sharma, Kabir Khadka and Nischal Khadka among others. Before filming, the artist was given a workshop by artist Sarita Giri for a month. More than 50 percent of the technicians in the film are from India. The film is being made under the joint banner of Gautam Productions and AB International. The film is co-produced by Amit Basnet, Ajit Kumar Thapa, and Pari Rana.
